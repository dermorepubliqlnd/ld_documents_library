1
PURPOSE

To establish a standardized process for documenting completion of approved external training

and verifying proof of knowledge transfer, ensuring that external learning outcomes are captured,

shared, traceable, and reflected in employee and training records.

2
SCOPE

2.1. In Scope

This SOP covers:

Documentation of completed external training programs delivered by third-party

providers, including seminars, workshops, certifications, conferences, and externally

hosted courses.

Submission and validation of proof of training completion, such as certificates,

attendance records, completion emails, digital badges, transcripts, or assessment

results.

Documentation of knowledge transfer activities conducted after external training,

where required by the External Training Policy, training approval conditions, or L&D

assessment.

Review, acceptance, record updating, and closure of post-training documentation

in the official ticketing and/or training record system.

Responsibilities of the requesting employee, immediate supervisor, L&D, HR, and

Department Head in ensuring completion of evidence and knowledge transfer

proof are complete and accurate.

2.2. Out of Scope

This SOP does not cover:

External training request submission, evaluation, approval, funding, and enrollment

processes.

Vendor sourcing, procurement, payment processing, reimbursement, or training

bond execution.

Design and delivery of internal training programs that are not linked to an

approved external training completion requirement.

Assessment of whether an employee should attend external training before

approval.

Performance management actions unrelated to training completion

documentation or knowledge transfer proof.

2.3. Process Start and End

The process begins when the employee completes an approved external training program

or receives completion evidence from the third-party provider.

The process ends when L&D verifies completion evidence and knowledge transfer proof, HR

or the designated records owner updates the employee training record, and the ticket or

record is closed.

3
REFERENCES

LD-POL-004-1.0-2026 - External Training

4
DEFINITIONS

External Training - Any learning intervention delivered by a third-party provider outside

the organization.

Proof of Completion - Documentary evidence that the employee attended, completed,

passed, or obtained recognition for the external training.

Knowledge Transfer - A structured activity where the trained employee shares relevant

learnings, tools, methods, or action points with appropriate internal stakeholders.

Proof of Knowledge Transfer - Evidence that knowledge transfer was planned, conducted,

attended, and documented, including materials, attendance, recordings, assessments, or

sign-offs.

Training Record - The official record maintained by L&D, HR, or the designated system

reflecting completed training and supporting documentation.

RICA Helpdesk - Requests, Incidents, & Change Assistant Helpdesk, or the official ticketing

system used to submit and track training-related documentation.

PROCESS FLOW DIAGRAM
7
PROCEDURE

7.1. Ticket Submission

The L&D Manager files a ticket on behalf of the employee within five (5) working days after

the employee completes the training or receives completion documentation from the

provider.

7.2. Submit Proof of Completion

7.2.1. The employee must complete the External Training Feedback Form, which captures

training relevance, key learning points, immediate application opportunities, and

recommended follow-up actions.

7.2.2. The employee attaches proof of external training completion to the ticket. Acceptable

proof of completion may include:

Certificate of completion, attendance, passing, or certification;

Provider-issued attendance report, completion email, transcript, digital

badge, or examination result;

Screenshot or system-generated completion record from the provider

platform, provided that required details are visible;

Other official provider confirmation accepted by L&D.

Completion evidence must clearly show, where available, the employee's name,

training title, provider name, completion or attendance date, training duration or hours,

and result or credential information.

For certifications, licensure, or programs with formal assessment results, the employee

shall include the result status and any credential validity period, renewal requirement, or

continuing education condition, when applicable.

This step must be completed within three (3) working days. Once completed, the ticket

is routed to Step 7.3.

7.3. Documentation Review

The L&D manager reviews the submitted proof of completion within five (5) working days

from submission. The review shall confirm whether the submitted document is legible,

complete, relevant to the approved training, and sufficient for recordkeeping.

If documentation is incomplete, unclear, or inconsistent with the approved training, the L&D

manager shall return the ticket to Step 7.2 with required corrections or additional evidence.

If documentation is valid and complete, the ticket is routed to Step 7.3.

7.4. Proof of Knowledge Transfer

The employee uploads proof of knowledge transfer within twenty (20) working days after

the activity. Acceptable proof may include one or more of the following, depending on the

approved method:

Knowledge Transfer Report or summary of topics covered, key insights, action

points, and recommendations;

Attendance sheet, participant list, or system attendance report;

Presentation deck, job aid, process guide, checklist, recording, or shared learning

material;

Participant feedback, acknowledgement, short quiz results, discussion notes, or

supervisor sign-off;

Evidence of application, such as updated work instruction, improvement proposal,

coaching log, or implementation action plan.

Proof of knowledge transfer must be sufficient to show that the learning was shared with

the intended audience and that participants had an opportunity to receive, discuss, and

apply the transferred knowledge.

The ticket is routed to Step 7.5 upon completion of this task.

If knowledge transfer is not required for a particular external training, then proceed to Step

7.6.

7.5. Validate Knowledge Transfer

L&D reviews proof of knowledge transfer within five (5) working days from submission.

Validation shall confirm that the activity was conducted, the target audience was covered,

materials or outputs were attached, and the proof supports the approved training

objectives.

If proof is incomplete, the L&D manager shall return the ticket to Step 7.4 for correction,

additional evidence, or rescheduling of the knowledge transfer activity, as applicable.

If the proof is valid and complete, the training record is updated, and the ticket is routed to

Step 7.6.1.

7.6. Employee Record Update and Closure

7.6.1. The HR Specialist acknowledges the submissions and updates employee records.

7.6.2. The employee adds their External Learning Record in Dermorepubliq University.

7.6.3. The employee verifies completion and closes the ticket.

9. KPI and COMPLIANCE

The following KPI is established to monitor the timeliness, completeness, and compliance of

external training completion documentation and knowledge transfer proof:

KPI / Metric Target Frequency Responsible Monitoring Tool

On-time Processing Rate ≥ 90% Quarterly L&D RICA Helpdesk Reports

(requests completed within SLA)

Failure to submit complete documentation or proof of knowledge transfer may result in delayed

ticket closure, incomplete training records, escalation to the immediate supervisor or Department

Head, and other actions in accordance with applicable policies. Repeated non-compliance may

be subject to management review.

Records shall be retained in the designated system or repository in accordance with applicable

HR and document retention requirements. Documents must be legible, traceable, and accessible

to authorized reviewers for audit and reporting purposes.

10.DOCUMENT REVIEW AND MAINTENANCE

The L&D Manager is responsible for maintaining and updating this SOP in coordination with HR

and relevant stakeholders. This SOP shall be reviewed annually or as needed due to changes in

the External Training Policy, documentation requirements, ticketing system workflow, audit

findings, or organizational structure. All revisions must be approved, documented, communicated,

and stored in the official document repository.
