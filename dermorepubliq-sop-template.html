Onboarding Program Facilitation and Administration

Department
Learning & Development
Document Owner
L&D Director
Responsible Dept.
Human Resources
Classification
Internal Use Only
1
Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure defines the process for facilitating, administering, and evaluating the Dermorepubliq employee onboarding program to ensure a consistent, engaging, and effective experience for all new hires across all departments and roles.

2
Scope

This procedure applies to:

  • All new regular and probationary employees across Dermorepubliq Corporation
  • L&D Supervisors, Trainers, and Content Developers facilitating onboarding sessions
  • HR Business Partners coordinating new hire schedules and documentation
3
Procedure
📌
Before You Begin Confirm the new hire's start date with HR at least 5 business days in advance. Ensure Litmos enrollment and system access are ready before Day 1.
  1. Receive new hire details from HR via the onboarding intake form no later than 5 business days before the start date.
  2. Enroll the new hire in the assigned Litmos onboarding learning path based on their role and department.
  3. Prepare facilitation materials including welcome kit, schedule, and department-specific modules.
  4. Conduct the Day 1 Orientation session covering company culture, values, and compliance requirements.
  5. Monitor e-learning completion via Litmos dashboard throughout the 30-day onboarding window.
  6. Conduct the 30-day check-in with the new hire and their immediate supervisor to assess onboarding effectiveness.
  7. Administer the post-onboarding evaluation form and encode results in the L&D tracker within 3 business days.
4
Definitions
  • Onboarding: The structured process of integrating a new employee into Dermorepubliq, covering orientation, role-specific training, and cultural assimilation.
  • Litmos: The company's Learning Management System used to deliver, track, and report on employee training.
  • L&D Tracker: The Notion-based project and task management system used by the L&D team.
